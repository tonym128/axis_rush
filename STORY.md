# AXIS RUSH 2026: THE GREAT CYLINDER TRIALS

## The Setting: 2026 and Beyond
The year is 2026. Humanity has long since abandoned the scorched surface of Earth for the massive, spinning cylindrical colonies known as the **Axis Habitations**. These megastructures, stretching hundreds of miles across the Lagrange points, are the last bastions of civilization—and the ultimate playgrounds for the elite.

Inside these massive tubes, gravity is a luxury managed by rotation. But on the outside, in the cold vacuum of space, the laws of physics are merely suggestions. This is where the **Axis Rush** was born.

## The Conflict: The Three Pillars
The solar system is locked in a cold war between three dominant factions, each vying for control of the "Source Code"—a mythical quantum encryption key said to control the life support systems of every Axis colony.

1.  **THE HEGEMONY:** The remnants of Earth's old-world military-industrial complex. They rule the Inner Axis with an iron fist, utilizing "pardon programs" to recruit the most dangerous pilots into their corporate-sponsored racing teams. For them, the Rush is a recruitment tool and a display of overwhelming force.
2.  **THE UNDERGROUND:** A loose collective of hackers, rebels, and scavengers living in the vents and sub-levels of the colonies. They see the Rush as a way to fund the resistance and potentially deliver a "Freedom Virus" directly into the Hegemony's central servers via the racing hubs.
3.  **THE VOID:** A transhumanist cult operating from the dark side of the asteroid belt. Comprised of cyborgs, stealth operatives, and those who have "deleted" their pasts, they believe the Axis Habitations are merely a simulation. They race not for credits or glory, but to find glitches in the architecture and transcend physical reality.

## The Sport: Cylindrical Racing
The Axis Rush is the most watched event in the system. Pilots must navigate tracks built both inside and outside the habitation cylinders. 

*   **Inside the Tube:** High-density air provides lift and cooling, but the walls are crowded with civilian infrastructure and defensive turrets.
*   **Outside the Tube:** Zero-drag vacuum allows for extreme speeds, but pilots are exposed to solar radiation and the constant threat of drifting into the infinite dark.

Victory in the Axis Rush League isn't just about speed—it's about survival. The winner of the 2026 Championship will be granted "Root Access," a privilege that could reshape the future of humanity or end it forever.
